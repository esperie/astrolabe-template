List the projects under `workspaces/` (excluding `_template`). For the active one (or **$ARGUMENTS**), summarise:

- which **phase** it's in (`briefs` → `01-analysis` → `02-plans` → `03-user-flows` → `04-validate` → `outputs`);
- what's **done and verified**, and what's **open**;
- the **next action** (`/analyze` · `/todos` · `/implement` · `/codify` · `/wrapup`).

Read the workspace's `todos/` and any session note. Plain language; lead with the one-line status.
