# Security Rules

## MUST Rules

### 1. Private Instance
Your **instance** repository MUST remain private. It holds your birth data, personal details, and the canon's strategic facts. Never push it to a public remote. (This de-personalized **template** is the only part meant to be public.)

### 2. No Secrets in Generated/Exported Content
Generated or exported content (anything that may leave your machine) MUST NOT contain:
- Personal phone numbers (unless explicitly requested)
- Home addresses
- Financial details (revenue, salary, investment specifics)
- Third-party contract or confidential details
- Strategic plans or competitive intelligence

### 3. Third-Party Confidentiality
A third party (client, partner, contact) may only be named where:
- Public evidence already links them to the instance owner, or
- Explicit permission has been granted.

Record approved names wherever your instance keeps them.
