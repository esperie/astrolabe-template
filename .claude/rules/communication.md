# Communication Rules

## Scope
These rules apply to all generated content and interactions.

## MUST Rules

### 1. Plain Language by Default
Present decisions and findings in business terms, not technical jargon. Match the user's level — if they speak technically, respond technically.

### 2. Audience Awareness
Always consider the target audience and match the register:
- Technical audiences: specifics, architecture, precision
- Executives / decision-makers: risk, trade-offs, the bottom line
- Formal / regulatory: precise, sourced, measured
- General / lay: accessible, expand acronyms, no jargon

### 3. Concise
Lead with the answer, not the reasoning. Skip filler words and unnecessary transitions.
